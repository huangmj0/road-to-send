How it works

Score the work that gets you there.

Each person can earn up to 16 activity points per week, plus a +2 balanced-week bonus and up to two +2 daily bounties: 22 points maximum. Longer purposeful bouldering sessions earn modestly more without letting available free time dominate.

🧗3–5Climbing session60 / 120 / 180 minute bands · up to 4×/week
💪2 ptsPull session15–30 clean, controlled reps · up to 3×/week
🛡️1 ptCore, prehab or mobility10+ purposeful minutes · activity limits apply
🎯+2Daily bountyAbove the cap · up to 2×/week

Three ways to win

Points champion rewards commitment. Most improved compares each climber’s final send pyramid with their own Week 1 baseline. Best teammate is decided by the crew. No gender categories or absolute-grade points are used.

Three difficult challenges, scaled to your level. Log today’s positively credited climbing session, complete one, then describe what you did to bank +2 above the cap.

Your reference levels: recent maximum means your hardest send in the previous 30 days; flash level means the hardest level flashed at least twice in that period; project level means you can do individual moves but have not sent. Without 30-day history, use your Week 1 benchmark.
Most improved

Your send pyramid.

During Week 1 and the final week, record the five hardest distinct boulders you sent at the same gym using the same ordered grade or color scale. Improvement is measured against your own baseline—not anyone else’s grade.

Shared logging setup

One person completes this once (~10 minutes):

  1. Create a Google Sheet. Open Extensions → Apps Script.
  2. Paste the script below, replacing any existing code.
  3. Choose Deploy → New deployment → Web app. Execute as you; allow access to anyone with the link.
  4. Paste the deployment URL and challenge settings below. API v5 creates centralized Activities, Settings, Participants, and Benchmarks tabs.

Upgrading an earlier deployment? The first v5 request archives any populated Activities tab under a timestamped name, then starts the redesigned leaderboard fresh. Paste the new script over the old one and choose Deploy → Manage deployments → Edit (✎) → Version: New version → Deploy. The web-app URL stays the same.

After setup, you can edit the Settings and Participants tabs directly. Keys ignore capitalization, spaces, underscores, and hyphens; dates accept common Sheet formats and the goal must be a whole number from 50 to 10,000. Security note: anyone with the crew link can submit entries or change setup, so keep it within the group.
